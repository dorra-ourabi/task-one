Habits related with food :

Adopt few habits that will help you eat food high in proteins, mineral waters and vitamins that your body needs to keep you in shape and help you balance a life without risks of health problems.


  1. Choose whole foods over processed foods whenever possible
  2. Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your meals
  3. Opt for lean protein sources, such as chicken, fish, beans, and tofu
  4. Limit your intake of added sugars and saturated fats
  5. Drink plenty of water throughout the day

Adding to this healthy eating schedule,if you would like to follow a strict diet there a few diets that are worldwidely famous, you will hear people offently say:


"I can follow whatever diet I like "

Well that's entirely wrong as you should visit a doctor or a nutrionist and he gets to choose the diet that fits best with your health conditions .Here's a list of helpful diets that you might ask your doctor about:

  • The ketogenic diet or shortly the keto diet:By restricting the total caloric intake, the body is forced to metabolize fat in place of carbohydrate. The ketogenic diet mimics a starvation or fasting state by denying the body the carbohydrate it requires to function normally, and forcing it to metabolize fat. As the fat is metabolized ketone bodies are produced.Here's a guide on how to follow it:
    A beginner's guide to keto diet
  • The intermittent fasting:Intermittent fasting is an eating plan that switches between fasting and eating on a regular schedule. Research shows that intermittent fasting is a way to manage your weight and prevent — or even reverse — some forms of disease.Here's a guide on how to follow it:
    The intermittent fasting guide
  • Veganism:While it is considered as a diet, where followers avoid animal-based products, some consider it a lifestyle (many vegans avoid wearing any leather or animal hide). A few years ago, vegans would only eat beans, legumes, grains, egg-free pasta, fruits, and vegetables. But advances in technology have allowed the alternative meat industry have provided vegans access to a wider variety of products. Here is a website to learn more abou it:
    The vegan diet
